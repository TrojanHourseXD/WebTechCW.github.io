Scrambled Eggs

Classic scrambled eggs, perfect for a quick and easy breakfast.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
2 minutes 3-5 minutes 5-7 minutes
Amount Served
1-2 people

Equipment Needed:

  • Nonstick skillet
  • Fork or whisk
  • Spatula

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, beat the eggs with a fork or whisk until they are well mixed.
  2. Melt the butter or heat the oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs to the skillet.
  3. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs as they cook. Continue stirring until the eggs are set but still slightly runny.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and season the eggs with salt and pepper, to taste.
  5. Transfer the scrambled eggs to a plate and serve hot.
