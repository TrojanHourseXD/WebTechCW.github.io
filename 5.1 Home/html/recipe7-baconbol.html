Bacon Bolognese

Rich and hearty bacon bolognese, perfect for a cozy dinner.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 minutes 45 minutes 1 hour
Amount Served
4-6 people

Equipment Needed:

  • Large pot
  • Large skillet
  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Wooden spoon

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chopped bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside on a paper towel-lined plate.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  4. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, red wine, tomato paste, sugar, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  6. Simmer the bolognese for 30-40 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  7. Serve the bolognese over the cooked spaghetti, topped with the crispy bacon and grated Parmesan cheese.
