Fried Chicken

Crispy and delicious fried chicken, perfect for a family dinner.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
30 minutes 30 minutes 1 hour
Amount Served
4-6 people

Equipment Needed:

  • Large pot or deep fryer
  • Tongs
  • Baking sheet
  • Paper towels

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken pieces in a large bowl and pour the buttermilk over them. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 24 hours.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk and let the excess drip off. Coat each piece in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  4. In a large pot or deep fryer, heat the oil to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Add the chicken pieces, a few at a time, and fry for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Use tongs to remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a baking sheet lined with paper towels to drain.
  7. Serve hot.
