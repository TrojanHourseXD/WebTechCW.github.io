Chicken Soup

Warm and comforting chicken soup, perfect for a cold day.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 minutes 1 hour 30 minutes 1 hour 45 minutes
Amount Served
6 people

Equipment Needed:

  • Large pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Stirring spoon
  • Ladle

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup egg noodles

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken, water, onion, carrots, celery, bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, uncovered, for 1 hour.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool slightly. Discard the skin and bones, and shred the meat. Return the meat to the pot.
  4. Add the egg noodles to the pot and simmer for an additional 10-12 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
  5. Remove the bay leaf and serve hot.
