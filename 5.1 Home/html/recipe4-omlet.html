Omelet

A classic and customizable breakfast dish.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 minutes 5 minutes 10 minutes
Amount Served
1 serving

Equipment Needed:

  • Nonstick skillet
  • Spatula
  • Bowl
  • Fork or whisk

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk or water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Optional fillings: diced ham, saut√©ed mushrooms, diced tomatoes, sliced onions, chopped herbs

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk or water, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter and let it melt, swirling to coat the bottom of the skillet.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and use a spatula to spread it evenly across the bottom of the skillet.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for 1-2 minutes, or until the bottom is set and the top is still slightly runny.
  5. Use a spatula to fold the omelet in half, adding any desired fillings and cheese to one side of the omelet as you fold it.
  6. Cook the omelet for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the eggs are cooked through.
  7. Slide the omelet onto a plate and serve hot, garnished with additional herbs if desired.
